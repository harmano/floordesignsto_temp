CONTACT

Floor Designs Toronto

With vast industry experience in vinyl, Floor Designs Toronto specializes in architectural film design and installation, for commercial and residential purposes. Our dedication to quality, innovation, and value based service has set us apart, and allowed Floor Designs Toronto to become the first mover and industry leader. With an internal design team, we provide swift design proofs, greater design flexibility and a quicker turnover time. We aim to push the boundaries of design and implementation with the aid of vinyl. With vinyl replacing the use of various raw materials, we provide a sustainable alternative that uses significantly less material while simultaneously providing the same end result.

We offer a variety of services spanning in the space of merging beautiful designs and vinyl film. From, wall vinyl, door vinyl, bathroom floor and shower vinyl, floor vinyl, door frame vinyl, countertop vinyl, seasonal vinyl designs and anywhere else where vinyl can be utilized! Floor Designs Toronto takes on only a few projects each year, allowing our master installer to be present during all projects, ensuring an industry best level of service.

Based in the Greater Toronto Area, Headquartered in Brampton, Ontario.
