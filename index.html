Types of Popular Vinyl Finishes.

Transform any smooth non-porous surface with one of our 1300 architectural vinyl film patterns, including natural wood, stone, marble, metals, leathers, fabrics, and many others. The multilayer film structure completely imitates patterns and textures of real finishes. Architectural film is self-adhering, can be installed on flat and curved surfaces (walls, ceilings, elevator cabs, doors, furniture, etc.), indoors and outdoors, and has a proven track record of durability.
